About

Done Right is a startup company that aims to tailor events specifically to each and every client’s needs. The company has been established for close to 5 years and has a portfolio of huge clients such as the Singapore Nights Festival, movie premiere for “Avengers: Infinity war” and the Great Eastern’s Women Run.

  • 2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our business started in mid 2015. Our team of consultants has experience that range from multinational corporations to small and medium-sized enterprises which gives our events planning a wide range of perspective, be it huge or little. Further, our team has a combined experience in the field of publicity and events planning to assure our clients that they are dealing with one of the best in the industry.

  • 2016

    Our First Event

    The Singapore Night Festival 2016 (SNF) has kicked off, lighting up our arts and heritage precinct with spectacular light art installations, performances and programmes created by local and international acts. The SNF is one of the most anticipated outdoor night events in the region

  • 2017

    Great Eastern Women's Run

    One of the most highly attended women’s only events in Singapore, the annual Great Eastern Women’s Run has a long and proud tradition to uphold. Te race took place on 12th November and event registration was launched on 19th July 2017 at One Raffles Place.

  • 2018

    Avengers: Infinity war

    Avengers: Infinity War is a 2018 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero team the Avengers, produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. It is the sequel to The Avengers (2012) and Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015), and the 19th film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).
